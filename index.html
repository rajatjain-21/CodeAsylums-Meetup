Think | Innovate | Hack

Now is the time to finally solve some of those problems that have been driving the industry crazy, but haven’t made it onto anyone’s individual roadmap.

Team up, bring a solution to life, and release it into the wild!

Great Speakers

Insightful tech talks by leaders and subject matter experts in the industry, helping you out with the challenges you may face and how to rise from those. A great chance to learn from the best

Networking

A great opportunity to meet and exchange ideas and thought processes with other like minded developers and professionals in the industry, an opportunity to network and learn how they approach a problem.

New People

Get a chance to work in a diverse team environment and make new friends, indulge in deep talks and discussions and give your imagination wings

Have Fun

Amazing fun events lined up where tech geeks come together to create something useful. There will be a lot of code, tons of food, no sleep and lots of caffeine.

Course of Events ↓

  • June 8th, 2019 (Sat)

    Ideation Phase

    Entrepreneurs will visit our premises with their startup ideas and pitch them infront of our students. Each team will choose an idea to work upon.
  • June 8th - June 14th, 2019 (Sat to Fri)

    Implementation Phase

    After the idea is selected, each team will be given a week's time to work on it and create a basic MVP (Minimal Viable Product) for the idea. In this time period, you will be in constant touch with the team so that they are on right track with the idea.
  • June 15th - June 16th, 2019 (Sat - Sun)

    Product Phase - Hackathon

    In these two days, the teams will be aiming towards building a market-ready beta version of the product. There will be intense brain-storming sessions with experts in the industry and thus the teams will be all set to build the final product
